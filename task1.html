TAJMAHAL

The Taj Mahal is a white marble mausoleum in Agra, India,
built by Mughal emperor Shah Jahan as a tomb for his wife,
Mumtaz Mahal, and is a UNESCO World Heritage Site,
known as a symbol of love and a masterpiece of Mughal architecture.

The Lighthouse of Alexandria,
also known as the Pharos of Alexandria,
